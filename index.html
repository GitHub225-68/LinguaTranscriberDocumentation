Welcome to The Lingua Transcriber Documentation

This guide will help you add, use, and remove the Lingua Transcriber app developed by VoxLinx Labs.

Adding the App

To add the Lingua Transcriber app to your Zoom account, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Zoom account.
  2. Go to the Apps section in the settings.
  3. Click on the "Add App" button.
  4. Search for the Lingua Transcriber app by name or VoxLinx Labs by company.
  5. Click on the "Install" button to add the app to your account.

Using the App

  1. Once you have added the Lingua Transcriber app, anything spoken in a language other than English will be translated into English.
  2. The translated text will then be sent as a text message in the chat.

Removing the App

If you no longer want to use the Lingua Transcriber app, you can remove it from your Zoom account:

  1. Log in to your Zoom account.
  2. Go to the Apps section in the settings.
  3. Locate the Lingua Transcriber app in the list of installed apps.
  4. Click on the "Uninstall" or "Remove" button next to the Lingua Transcriber app.
  5. Optionally, please let us know the reason for uninstalling the app to help us improve.
